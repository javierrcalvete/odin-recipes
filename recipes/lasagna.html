Lasagna

Description

Zucchini slices step in for pasta in this low-carbs and gluten-free beef lasagna that is delicious and satisfying; such a crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 large zucchine
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beeg
  • 1 cups low car marinana sauce
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 egg
